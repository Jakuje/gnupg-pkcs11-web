gnupg-pkcs11 is a project to implement a BSD-licensed standard smart-card support in GnuPG.

We all disagree with Werner Koch's attitude towards PKCS#11 which is the de-facto standard to work with cryptographic tokens.

In short, we support gnupg-pkcs11-scd daemon as a drop-in replacement for the scd shipped with GnuPG.
